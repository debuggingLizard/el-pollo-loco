How to Play

Help Pepe find coins, defeat enemies on your journey and collect tabasco salsa to fight against the crazy hen!

Defeat enemies by squashing them under your feet as you move or by jumping on them. Collect coins and tabasco salsa bottles to throw at the crazy hen. You can only start throwing bottles after the crazy hen has woken up.
